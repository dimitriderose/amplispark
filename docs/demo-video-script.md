# Amplifi — Demo Video Script

**Target runtime: ~3:20 | Max: 4:00**
**Upload to: YouTube (public, English)**

---

## [0:00–0:14] THE PROBLEM

**Screen:** Black screen, then slow zoom into a cluttered browser tab bar — Canva, ChatGPT, Buffer, stock photo site, spreadsheet.

**Voiceover:**
"Every week, social media managers bounce between five tools — write captions here, find images there, schedule somewhere else. It's fragmented, slow, and the output never quite feels cohesive. What if one AI could generate your entire content package — captions, images, strategy — all at once, in a single stream?"

---

## [0:14–0:32] INTRODUCE AMPLIFI

**Screen:** Navigate to `amplifi-seimyaykpa-uc.a.run.app`. Landing page visible. Click "Get Started."

**Voiceover:**
"This is Amplifi. An AI creative director that builds complete, platform-ready content packages — not sequentially, but all together, the way a real creative team would think."

**Screen:** Onboarding wizard — Step 1: Business Info form. Type a URL into the website field.

**Voiceover:**
"Setup takes ninety seconds. Drop in your website URL and Amplifi reads your brand — pulling your voice, your audience, your positioning — automatically."

---

## [0:32–0:55] ONBOARDING WIZARD

**Screen:** Watch the brand analysis run. Fields populate: industry, tone of voice, target audience.

**Voiceover:**
"It analyzed the site and extracted the brand identity. No manual setup."

**Screen:** Step 2 — Brand preferences. Show visual style selector, tone sliders.

**Voiceover:**
"You refine the details — visual style, content tone — and select your platforms."

**Screen:** Step 3 — Platform selection grid. Click Instagram, LinkedIn, X. Click "Generate Calendar."

**Voiceover:**
"Then you pick your platforms. Amplifi knows each one has different optimal frequencies, formats, and audiences. Instagram gets carousels. LinkedIn gets thought leadership. X gets punchy hooks."

---

## [0:55–1:45] THE CORE — INTERLEAVED GENERATION

**Screen:** Content calendar view loading. A 7-day grid appears. Click on a day to start generation.

**Voiceover:**
"Here's what makes Amplifi different."

**Screen:** The generation panel opens. Show the output stream in real time — a caption line appears, then immediately below it an image starts rendering, then another caption beat, then the image completes.

**Voiceover:**
"Most AI tools generate text, then separately generate an image. Amplifi uses Gemini's interleaved output — the caption and the image are created in the same stream, each one informing the other. The visual and the copy are built together, not bolted together afterward."

**Screen:** Pause on the completed post. Show the caption alongside the generated image.

**Voiceover:**
"The result is content where the image actually matches the caption — because they were never separate thoughts."

**Screen:** Click on an Instagram carousel slot. Show slides generating in parallel — each slide's image and caption appearing together.

**Voiceover:**
"For carousels, Amplifi generates all slides in parallel. Four slides, four captions, four images — simultaneously. A full carousel in under thirty seconds."

---

## [1:45–2:05] PLATFORM BREADTH + SCORING

**Screen:** Scroll the 7-day calendar. Show different platform posts — LinkedIn, X, TikTok. Click on a post to show its quality score breakdown.

**Voiceover:**
"Eleven platforms supported. And every piece of content gets scored across five dimensions — hook strength, relevance, CTA effectiveness, platform fit, teaching depth — weighted differently per platform, because what works on LinkedIn doesn't work on TikTok."

**Screen:** Show the score and dimension breakdown briefly.

---

## [2:05–2:25] VOICE STRATEGY COACH

**Screen:** Click the Voice Coach button. Voice coach panel opens. Speak: "Which post should I lead with on Monday?"

**Screen:** Waveform animation shows the AI listening, then responding in real-time audio.

**Voiceover:**
"The voice strategy coach is powered by Gemini Live — a real-time conversation. Ask about your calendar, request a tone adjustment, get a second opinion on a headline. It knows your full content context."

**Screen:** Brief back-and-forth — one follow-up question, one answer.

---

## [2:25–2:38] AI MEDIA EDITOR + VEO VIDEO

**Screen:** Click edit on an image. Type: "Make the lighting warmer and add a subtle bokeh background." Image updates.

**Voiceover:**
"Every generated image is editable in natural language — directly in the app."

**Screen:** Click "Generate Video Clip" on a post. Short Veo 3.1 clip plays.

**Voiceover:**
"And for video content, Veo generates short clips from the same creative brief."

---

## [2:38–2:52] EXPORT + INTEGRATIONS

**Screen:** Click Export dropdown — show Notion sync, email export, bulk download ZIP options. Click Notion sync — success toast.

**Voiceover:**
"When the calendar's ready, sync to Notion, email the package to the team, or bulk-download everything. Amplifi fits into existing workflows — it doesn't replace them."

---

## [2:52–3:08] ARCHITECTURE

**Screen:** Architecture diagram (PNG). Highlight the interleaved generation path.

**Voiceover:**
"Under the hood: FastAPI on Cloud Run, Firestore for state, Cloud Storage for assets, all provisioned with Terraform. The interleaved stream comes from Gemini 3.1 Flash Image Preview through the Google ADK — text and image tokens in a single response. The voice coach uses Gemini 2.5 Flash Native Audio over a WebSocket."

**Screen:** Hold diagram for 3–4 seconds so viewers can read it.

---

## [3:08–3:22] CLOSE

**Screen:** Return to the app. Full 7-day calendar, populated with content — images, captions, scores. Slow scroll across the week.

**Voiceover:**
"A week of platform-native content — captions, images, video clips, scores, strategy — in the time it used to take to write one caption. That's Amplifi."

**Screen:** URL fades in: `amplifi-seimyaykpa-uc.a.run.app`

**Screen:** Fade to black. Amplifi wordmark centered.
